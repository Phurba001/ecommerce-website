Profitch Social Ventures invests in early-stage companies that create jobs for extremely poor communities in Nepal. Our mission is to help these companies grow and create more jobs for families that need them most, so that they can come out of extreme poverty. We provide seed funding and advisory support in critical areas to scale small and growing businesses and create meaningful impact.
